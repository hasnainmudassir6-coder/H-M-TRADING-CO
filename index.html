About H M Trading Co.

Established in 2014 in Cuttack, Odisha, H M Trading Co. is a dedicated B2B supplier specializing in the procurement and distribution of industrial, electrical, and mechanical engineering materials.


We serve cement plants, EPC contractors, and infrastructure developers by ensuring seamless access to essential materials. Our operations focus on sourcing from trusted manufacturers to minimize delays and optimize costs for our partners.
